drama, fantasy

Year of production: September 4, 2025

Director: Duman Yerkimbek

Cast: Zhasulan Kopbergen, Ermek Shinbolatov, Almat Sakatov, Shugyla Yeshenova

About the film

A city teenager, sent to his grandmother’s village after failing an exam, discovers he is trapped in a time loop where each day ends with a catastrophic dam burst. Realizing each cycle gives him a chance to prevent the disaster and save his loved ones, he must find a way to break free and change his perspective on life.
